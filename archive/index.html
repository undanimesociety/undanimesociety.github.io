Anime Society will be holding screening this summer every other Wednesday starting May 16, 2007. Come in from the summer heat and mosquitoes and enjoy some anime. We’re also looking at going to Ai-kon (www.Ai-kon.org) in Winnipeg July 27-29.

Just who are we?

If you want a serious existential question answered, you might wish to seek elsewhere, otherwise we are the North Dakota Anime Society, based out of the University of North Dakota. The purpose of the UND Anime Society to promote an understanding and interest in Japanese animation in the surrounding community. At least that's what it says on the Student Organization website.
