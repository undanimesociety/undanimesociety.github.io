ADV's Perfect Collection Flawed!

ADV has added a new title to its reduced-price Perfect Collection line, now making the line no longer "Perfect." Martian Successor Nadesico and all of its six-disc glory is to be released September 24, 2002 at $89.98.

Sailor Yen Power! Make Up!

According to the good folks at Anime Daisuki, a sixth season of Sailor Moon is in the works. While not confirmed, it is rumored to believe this will not only please die-hard fans, but the people behind the production and whatnot of the series because as we learned a while back, Sailor Moon equals money.
